Elks

Elk's Software Development strives to create dynamic, responsive content that represents the goals and values of our esteemed clientele.

It is Our mission to understand the needs of our customers in order to help their business grow.

Utilizing ambitious marketing approaches, strategic analytics and performance driven business solutions, to elevate your company's Brand Awareness, Community Engagements, & Productivity with the help of our intelligent systems engineers.

We are here to help

Beyond the Elk's Software Development Mission Statement; the members of the Elks choose to conduct the bulk of our development through Webstorm, PHPStorm, and Visual Studio Code.

Additionally, we Communicate through Discord messages and in-person meetings. Our team meets at 11am on Tuesdays and Thursdays after class. Availabilities have been discussed and correspondence through group chats have been arranged.

It is agreed upon that all Conflicts will first be addressed with team members not involved in the conflict and each one of us agrees to help resolve Conflicts between other members. If we still can't agree as a team, outside mediation will be sought after. Our goal is to share in the workload and learn together. Through the merits of collaboration and cooperation, we as Elks will inform each other if we are struggling and believe it is in everyone's favor to assist each other in whichever way we can.

Upon retrospective analysis of this Working Agreement we have decided that meetings conducted routinely on wednesdays at 8am is key to our success.
